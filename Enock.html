About Me

Biostatistician & Web Developer

I am a dedicated Biostatistician and Web Developer with a strong foundation in statistical modeling, data analysis, and modern web technologies. My passion lies in using data to solve real-world problems in health, research, and public policy—while building interactive platforms that make complex information accessible and actionable. With advanced skills in R, SAS, SPSS, Python, and statistical methodologies, I specialize in designing and conducting data analyses for clinical trials, epidemiological studies, and public health research. I’m proficient in data cleaning, survival analysis, regression modeling, and data visualization—ensuring accurate, reproducible, and meaningful results that inform scientific and policy decisions. Complementing my statistical expertise is my ability to develop user-friendly web applications and dashboards using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Django. I create tools that allow researchers, organizations, and decision-makers to interact with and interpret data effortlessly. My interdisciplinary approach bridges the gap between data science and technology—empowering evidence-based solutions through both advanced analytics and intuitive digital tools.
